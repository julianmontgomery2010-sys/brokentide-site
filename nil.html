BrokenTide NIL

Project NIL

NIL is a melee combat game set in post-apocalyptic 2079 industrial japan. Fight your way through a world infested by advanced machines, and pelted with endless rain wielding nothing but your trusty umbrella.

Story

When the climate hit a tipping point and population started to drop due to extreme weather conditions as a last ditch effort humanity created the NIL, and while the NIL did help with improving the state of the earth they also targeted humans as a threat.
